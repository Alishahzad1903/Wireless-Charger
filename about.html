About us

We're on a mission to make electric vehicle (EV) charging more convenient, efficient, and eco-friendly. Our app monitors and optimizes wireless EV chargers, offering hassle-free charging solutions for a greener world.

Our Vision

We believe in the power of EVs to combat climate change. However, limited battery energy density poses challenges. We're dedicated to making frequent and convenient charging the norm for EV drivers.

Wireless Charging Simplified

Our app leverages wireless power transfer (WPT), particularly inductive power transfer (IPT), for tangle-free, user-friendly charging. No cords, no hassle.

Unlocking EV Potential

We're exploring EVs as mobile power sources for the grid and homes (V2G and V2H). Bidirectional wireless charging is part of our vision.

Cutting-Edge Tech

Our current project: a game-changing 22 kW Nanocrystalline IPT system for efficient, high-density wireless EV charging. We're pushing the boundaries.

Our Impact

Beyond science, our tech enhances user convenience and reduces energy consumption. Join us in driving a cleaner, sustainable future.
